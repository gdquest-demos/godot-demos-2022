Ranged attacks

This Godot project contains three demos related to ranged attacks:

  1. The main scene (res://Demo.tscn) shows ranged attacks with and without area of damage effects.
  2. The HomingDemo scene (res://homing_demo/HomingDemo.tscn) shows how to make homing projectiles, like homing missiles.
  3. The AmmoReloadingDemo scene (res://ammo_and_reloading_demo/AmmoReloadingDemo.tscn) focuses on managing ammo and reloading weapons.
